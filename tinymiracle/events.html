Our 5 Pillars




Social Awareness


When life in poverty is all you know, every day tends to be a struggle, and priorities are more commonly organized based on survival. To overcome this and give people the support they need, we cover over 150 topics during awareness sessions, ranging from financial literacy and the confidence to speak up about domestic abuse to hygiene and human rights. An empowered mindset is a capable mindset, and that shift is what we rally behind.




Best Varieties of Wheat for Rabi Season

1. Hi 8759 (Puja Tejas)

One of the varieties of wheat has a high level of rust resistance. It is a high yielding durum wheat variety with an average yield of >5.7t/ha and potential yield of 7.6t/ha, was released for timely sown irrigated conditions of Central Zone (CZ) by the Central Sub- Committee on Crop Standards, Notification and Release of Varieties for Agricultural Crops.
